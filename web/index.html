About This App

Project Story

RFM began with a question about the monthly return from a ₹1,00,000 bond and whether it was safer to begin with ₹25,000. The discussion expanded into bonds, FDs, Government schemes, taxation, ITR filing, and a simple financial companion.

Engineering Principles

  • Keep screens and language simple.
  • Important tasks within three clicks.
  • Explain every important term.
  • The app assists; the user decides.
