Filing Deadline

ITR-1 (Sahaj) is for individuals with pension/salary income, interest income, and one house property — with total income below ₹50 lakhs. This is the simplest form.

ITR-2 is for individuals with income from capital gains, foreign assets, or total income above ₹50 lakhs. More complex but required for these income types.

ITR-3 is required because you have consulting/business income. It covers all income types including business/profession.

⚠️ Important Reminder

Always cross-check your AIS (Annual Information Statement) against Form 26AS before filing. Any mismatch will trigger a notice from the Income Tax Department. File on the official portal: incometax.gov.in
